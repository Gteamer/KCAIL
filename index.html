Services

Advancing the development of AI and Machine Learning through research, education & collaboration. We believe in the ability of Machine Learning and AI to commoditize knowledge, automate labor, disrupt outdated and dysfunctional capital flows and transform the human experience. As data scientists, we come alongside and learn from our client’s expertise about their customers and their business. We will assemble a team with specific skills to quickly learn the things that make your business unique. Together we will chart a course to tackle the problems you wish to solve.

Data Science

Need to leverage data to gain critical business insights? Our data science team ties together advance Machine Learning algorithms with beautiful data visualization tools so you can extract critical insights for your organization.

AI Education & Training

Led by industry experts, our AI training program will make you skilled in machine learning, deep learning, data wrangling, and data visualization. Our curriculum was built to teach you everything you need to know to become an AI specialist.

AI Research & Development

Have a burning questions you think data and Machine Learning can answer? Have a disruptive idea that leverages AI? We offer end-to-end Machine Learning, Data Science, AI & software development.

Competencies

Natual Language Processing

Power your products with NLP features and turn emails, tweets, or any text data into actionable insights.

Computer Vision

Power your business applications with deep learning and computer vision technology to create a richer user experience.

Predictive Analytics

Adopt predictive modeling, machine learning, and data mining to transform an increasing amount of big data into business value.

Data Engineering

Data warehouse creation, data visualization, data wrangler, we handle all things data.
